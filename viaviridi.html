ViaViridi LCA Tool 2021

Client: ViaViridi
Role: Project leader

Overview

This project centred on creating a more accessible Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) tool for the construction industry. ViaViridi, a consultancy specialising in sustainable strategies, commissioned the development of a tool that would simplify both the LCA and Environmental Product Declaration (EPD) process for clients with limited technical expertise.

Process

As the project leader, I was responsible for stakeholder communication, led team meetings, and made critical decisions that shaped the research and design process. I guided the team through a flexible Double Diamond methodology, adapted to support agile and iterative development. Based on the analysis of existing tools and user needs, I identified key barriers related to cost, complexity, and language. These insights informed a concept focused on usability and accessibility, with particular attention to simplifying technical terminology and data workflows.

Outcome

The final outcome was a digital tool designed to streamline the LCA data collection and transfer process. I structured the interface using clear categories, simplified navigation, and built-in support features. The card-based layout allowed users to progress at their own pace and focus only on relevant input, making the process more approachable and efficient.
