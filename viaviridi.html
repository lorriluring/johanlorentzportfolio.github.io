ViaViridi LCA Tool 2021

Client: ViaViridi
Role: Project leader

Overview

The ViaViridi LCA Tool project focused on developing digital tools for sustainable design in the construction industry. The goal was to make life cycle assessment (LCA) accessible and actionable for architects and engineers.

Process

As project leader, I coordinated research, user interviews, and prototyping. We mapped user needs, tested early concepts, and iterated on the toolâ€™s interface to ensure clarity and usability for non-experts.

Outcome

The outcome was a user-friendly LCA tool prototype that helps construction professionals evaluate environmental impact early in the design process, supporting more sustainable decisions.

This project strengthened my skills in digital tool design, user research, and leading multidisciplinary teams toward sustainable innovation.
